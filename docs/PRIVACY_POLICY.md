# Privacy Policy for Biohacker

**Effective Date:** March 18, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 18, 2026

## Introduction

Biohacker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Biohacker mobile application (the "App").

This Policy complies with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the requirements of Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

**IMPORTANT MEDICAL DISCLAIMER:** Biohacker is designed for informational and tracking purposes only. It is NOT a medical device and does NOT provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals before starting, stopping, or modifying any health protocol or treatment.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information that you voluntarily provide:

- **Account Information:** Email address, name
- **Profile Data:** Age, height, weight, gender
- **Profile Photos:** Optional profile images stored securely in our cloud storage
- **Health Goals:** User-entered text describing your health and wellness objectives

### 1.2 Health Information

We collect health-related information that you choose to track:

- **Peptide Protocol Data:** Information about peptide cycles including dosage, frequency, duration, and route of administration
- **Laboratory Results:** PDF documents containing lab results and biomarker data that you upload
- **Extracted Biomarker Data:** Structured data extracted from your lab results using artificial intelligence processing
- **Health Metrics:** Any additional health tracking data you enter into the App

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** App features used, session duration, interaction patterns
- **Log Data:** Error reports, performance data, and diagnostic information

### 1.4 Authentication Data

When you use third-party authentication services:

- **Google Sign-In:** If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your email address and basic profile information (name, profile picture) as permitted by Google's authorization scope

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Primary Functions

- **App Functionality:** Provide core features including protocol tracking, lab result analysis, and health data visualization
- **Data Processing:** Process uploaded lab results using AI technology to extract and structure biomarker information
- **Personalization:** Customize your experience based on your profile and preferences
- **Progress Tracking:** Enable you to monitor your health optimization journey over time

### 2.2 Service Improvement

- **Quality Enhancement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve App performance and user experience
- **Bug Fixes:** Identify and resolve technical issues
- **Feature Development:** Understand user needs to develop new features and improvements

### 2.3 Communication

- **Service Notifications:** Send essential information about your account and App functionality
- **Support:** Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
- **Updates:** Inform you of important changes to our services or policies

### 2.4 Legal Compliance

- **Legal Obligations:** Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
- **Safety and Security:** Protect the rights, property, and safety of our users and the public

We may disclose your information when required by law or in response to:

- Requests from government or law enforcement agencies

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or prominent notice in the App before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

### 3.5 Aggregated and De-identified Data

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption:** Data encrypted at rest in our databases and in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher
- **Access Controls:** Strict access limitations ensuring only authorized personnel can access data
- **Authentication Security:** Secure password hashing and optional multi-factor authentication
- **Infrastructure Security:** Regular security audits, monitoring, and updates to our systems
- **Secure APIs:** Protected API endpoints with authentication and rate limiting

### 4.2 HIPAA Compliance

While Biohacker is not a covered entity under HIPAA, we implement HIPAA-aligned security practices:

- Administrative, physical, and technical safeguards for Protected Health Information (PHI)
- Business Associate Agreements with service providers handling health data
- Regular risk assessments and security training
- Incident response procedures for potential data breaches

### 4.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal or health information, we will:

- Notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery (GDPR requirement)
- Provide details about the breach, data affected, and remediation steps
- Report to relevant authorities as required by applicable law
- Take immediate steps to secure systems and prevent further unauthorized access

### 4.4 User Responsibility

You are responsible for:

- Maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials
- Using a strong, unique password for your account
- Notifying us immediately of any unauthorized access or security concerns
- Keeping your device secure with PIN, biometric, or password protection

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 GDPR Rights (European Economic Area Users)

If you are located in the EEA, you have the following rights under GDPR:

**Right to Access (Article 15):** Request a copy of your personal data we hold

**Right to Rectification (Article 16):** Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data

**Right to Erasure (Article 17):** Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")

**Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18):** Limit how we use your personal data

**Right to Data Portability (Article 20):** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

**Right to Object (Article 21):** Object to our processing of your personal data

**Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7):** Withdraw consent at any time where we rely on consent

**Right to Lodge a Complaint:** File a complaint with your local data protection authority

**Legal Basis for Processing:** We process your data based on:
- Your consent (for health data tracking)
- Performance of a contract (providing App services)
- Legitimate interests (improving our services, security)
- Legal compliance (when required by law)

### 5.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under CCPA:

**Right to Know:** Request disclosure of categories and specific pieces of personal information collected

**Right to Delete:** Request deletion of your personal information

**Right to Opt-Out of Sale:** We do NOT sell personal information (no opt-out necessary)

**Right to Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

**Authorized Agent:** You may designate an authorized agent to make requests on your behalf

**Response Timeline:** We will respond to verifiable consumer requests within 45 days

### 5.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:

1. **In-App Settings:** Access data export and account deletion through App Settings > Privacy
2. **Email Request:** Contact us at privacy@biohacker.app with your request
3. **Verification:** We may require verification of your identity before processing requests

We will respond to your request within:
- 30 days (GDPR)
- 45 days (CCPA)
- We may extend by an additional 30/45 days if necessary, with notification

### 5.4 Data Retention and Deletion

**Standard Retention:** We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services

**Account Deletion:** When you delete your account:
- Personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
- Some data may be retained in backup systems for up to 90 days
- Aggregated/de-identified data may be retained indefinitely
- Legal or regulatory requirements may necessitate longer retention

**Data Export:** Before deleting your account, you can export all your data in JSON format

## 6. Children's Privacy

**Age Restriction:** Users must be at least 13 years old to use the App

**Parental Consent:** Users aged 13-17 should have parental or guardian consent before using the App

**Verification:** We do not specifically verify user age, but we require age confirmation during registration

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@biohacker.app.

**NOT Medical Advice:** Biohacker is a tracking and informational tool only. It does NOT provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or professional healthcare services.

**No Doctor-Patient Relationship:** Use of the App does not create a doctor-patient relationship between you and Biohacker or any affiliated parties.
